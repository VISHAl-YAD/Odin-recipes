Chicken Tikka Recipes

Description

Chicken Tikka is a popular Indian dish that consists of marinated and grilled chunks of chicken, typically served with a flavorful blend of spices and yogurt.

The marination process imparts a rich and aromatic taste, while the grilling adds a smoky flavor to the tender chicken pieces. Chicken tikka is often enjoyed as a standalone appetizer or incorporated into various curries and dishes.

Ingredients

  1. Chicken: Boneless and skinless chicken pieces, cut into chunks
  2. Yogurt: Plain, thick yogurt for marination
  3. Spices: Common spices include cumin powder, coriander powder, turmeric, garam masala, and red chili powder
  4. Garlic and Ginger: Freshly minced or paste for flavor
  5. Lemon Juice: Adds a tangy element to the marinade
  6. Salt: To taste
  7. Cooking Oil: For greasing the grill or oven tray
  8. Skewers: Wooden or metal skewers for threading the chicken pieces
  9. Optional: Kasuri Methi (dried fenugreek leaves) or chaat masala for added flavor

These ingredients come together to create a flavorful and aromatic marinade for the chicken tikka.
